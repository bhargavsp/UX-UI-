Jersey

Jersey is a 2019 Telugu sports drama movie starring Nani, Shraddha Srinath, Sanusha and Sathyaraj. The story revolves around Arjun, a talented cricket player, who quits playing after he fails to get selected to the Indian cricket team because of internal politics. Ten years later, still without a job, the 36-year-old Arjun decides to start playing cricket again and make it to the Indian team, mostly for his son. In spite of his age, Arjun manages to get selected to the Ranji team. Will Arjun manage to find a spot in the Indian cricket team? Will his dream of playing for the country come true?

Kanam is a 2018 Telugu horror thriller movie starring Sai Pallavi, Naga Shourya, Veronika Arora, Gandhari Nithin and Santhana Bharathi.
